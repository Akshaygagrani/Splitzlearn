Competitive Coding

Home/Course Details

Objectives

This program will start your journey into an inspiring world of competitive coding. You can learn the widely used problem-solving methods, develop your statistical thinking capabilities, and test the limits of your reasoning ability. The course will cover Advanced Data Structures such as Segment Trees, Binary Index/Fenwick Trees. You can learn to build algorithms involving Dynamic Programming, Bitmasking, Greedy Algorithms, and Divide & Conquer. Other advanced competitive subjects will also be discussed in the course. This course is available to all coders looking forward to optimizing their problem-solving process and learning new algorithmic skills that can help them solve the problem quickly.

Eligibility

You should have a good knowledge of fundamentals (such as sorting, searching, recursion) and data structures (stacks, queues, linked lists, hashing, heaps, trees, graphs) along with problem-related space-time complexity analysis.
