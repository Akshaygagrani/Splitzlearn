About us

Learning with Love and Laughter

Splitzlearn is an ed-tech community run by IIT(ISM) Dhanbad Alumnus, who identified the need of an interactive learner friendly live training for people. It is known that learners are not much comfortable, learning and asking doubts in a large group specially with many unknown mates around. We allow learners to make their own classroom, choose an instructor of their choice and learn in their comfort pace, without paying exorbitant prices. At Splitzlearn we believe that a happy mind is the key for comprehensive learning. A classroom of well connected students, led by an experienced teacher triggers your the overall development.
